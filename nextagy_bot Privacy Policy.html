1. Introduction

Nextagy (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Nextagy Bot (“Service”), an AI-powered automation agent designed to integrate with Google services and streamline business workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, access, and protect user data when you connect your Google account to our Service.

2. Google User Data We Access

When you authorize Nextagy Bot via Google OAuth, we may request access to the following Google services:

  • Gmail: Read, send, modify, delete emails
  • Google Drive: View, upload, edit, delete files
  • Google Sheets: Read, create, update, delete spreadsheets
  • Google Calendar: View, create, edit, delete events
  • Google Forms: Read and manage responses
  • Basic profile information: Name, email address

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your Google data strictly to provide automation services, including:

  • Automating email workflows (reading, replying, organizing)
  • Managing files and documents in Google Drive
  • Updating and analyzing Google Sheets data
  • Scheduling and managing calendar events
  • Processing form submissions
  • Executing AI-powered workflows based on user instructions

We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Level of Access

Nextagy Bot may perform the following actions depending on your granted permissions:

  • Read Data: Access emails, files, spreadsheets, and events
  • Write Data: Create emails, files, sheets, and calendar entries
  • Modify Data: Update or organize existing content
  • Delete Data: Remove emails, files, or records when instructed

All actions are performed only based on user-triggered workflows or explicit automation rules.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • We store OAuth tokens securely using encryption.
  • Access to user data is restricted and controlled.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures.
  • We do not store unnecessary content from your Google account.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may share data only:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our legal rights
  • To comply with Google API Services User Data Policy

7. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with Google APIs. Your use of Google services is also subject to Google’s own policies.

8. User Control & Revocation

You can revoke access at any time by:

  • Visiting your Google Account permissions page
  • Disconnecting Nextagy Bot

Once revoked, we will no longer access your data.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Users may request deletion at any time.
